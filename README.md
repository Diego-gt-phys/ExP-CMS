# ExP-CMS
Experimental Collider Physics exercise. The objective is to find an artificial heavy Higgs boson signal in the channel H->ZZ->2l2j. The heavy comes from the assumption that the mH>2mZ. This study is aimed to replicade the studies carried by the CMS and ATLAS collaborations at the LHC before the discovery of the Higgs boson in 2012.

The data is Run I data captured at a CoM energy of 8TeV and Luminosity of 19.7fb^-1. A fake signal has been added and the objective is to find its properties
